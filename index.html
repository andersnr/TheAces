Band History

The Band formed in 2008 starting with the Ramirez sisters, Cristal and Alisa. They recruited members McKenna and Katie and formed the Blue Aces. They are now known as The aces and have 3 studio albums and 3 EP's released.

The Aces Albums

Best Songs

Lovin' Is Bible

Lovin' is bible was the first Aces song I ever heard. It is a song that I always find myself coming back to because it is just so good. It is happy for spring and summer but can also be an uplifting mood for fall and winter!

All Mean Nothing

This song is so amazing. I recently rediscovered All Mean Nothing and it is just one of those songs you can't necessarily relate to but you will sing it like you wrote it when you are alone in the car. A must listen, badass breakup song.

Girls Make Me Wanna Die

Amazing queer pop/rock song about liking someone who doesn't feel the same way. So amazing and so fun to see and dance to live. One of my favorite songs off their latest album.
